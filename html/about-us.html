What is

The Student College Committee (SCC) at Multimedia University (MMU) Cyberjaya, made up of 21 committee members, provides key services and accommodations to enhance the living experience of hostel residents. They manage room allocations, address maintenance issues, and ensure student welfare by acting as a bridge between students and the university administration. In addition to handling logistical needs, the SCC also organizes events to foster community and personal development, helping create a safe, comfortable, and engaging environment for all residents.
