What is

The Student College Committee (SCC) at Multimedia University (MMU) Cyberjaya was founded in 2022, following the COVID-19 lockdown, to support hostel residents and address the unique challenges of post-pandemic campus life. Comprising 21 members, the SCC manages room allocations, responds to maintenance needs, and ensures student welfare by acting as a key liaison between students and the university administration. In addition to handling logistical tasks, the committee organizes events that foster community and personal growth among residents. Through their continuous efforts, the SCC strives to maintain a safe, comfortable, and enriching living environment for all students on campus.

SCC Cyberjaya-Melaka Meeting, Jan 2024
