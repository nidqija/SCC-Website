Why Join Us?

Sports and Physical Activities!

Hostel residents at Multimedia University (MMU) Cyberjaya have access to enjoyable physical activities and sports programs, like as Zumba and aerobics classes, which are arranged by the Student College Committee (SCC). You will have a significant influence on student life as an SCC member by supporting these events and encouraging a thriving, healthy community among your peers.

Major Events and Celebrations!

Acquire front-row seats to the biggest parties and events on campus! As an SCC member, you will be invited to fun events hosted by the university that include delicious food, live entertainment, and exciting activities. It's an opportunity to make lifelong friendships, indulge in the best of MMU's vibrant campus culture, and make amazing experiences.
